Ben Adida is the Executive Director of VotingWorks, a non-profit building election technology everyone can trust. His passion is using technology to empower individuals. Ben has extensive experience in voting systems, identity, payments, health IT, security & privacy, and Web architecture. He has developed free software for more than 20 years and Web software for more than 25 years.

Previously, Ben was VP of Engineering at Clever, Director of Engineering at Square, Director of Engineering at Mozilla, Research Faculty at Harvard Medical School / Children's Hospital Boston, and research fellow with the Center for Research on Computation and Society at Harvard. Ben received his PhD from MIT's Cryptography and Information Security group.
